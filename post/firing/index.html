Firing Solution

Aggressively Boxy

Implements firing solution code from Ian Millington’s AI for Games. Boxes randomly throw themselves at the center disk with a sphere collider on it. This causes them to fly off after hitting. Every 5s, the box checkes if it has a solution with its current power. If not, it increases its power every check until it has one.
